# AnWeiJam

AnWeiJam is a social music sharing platform that allows users to connect over their favorite tracks, share music with followers, and view their personal Spotify listening statistics. 

## Features

- **Spotify Integration:** Connects with your Spotify account to display your personal listening stats, including your Top 10 Songs and Top 5 Artists.
- **Social Feed:** View a timeline of music posts. The home feed filters posts so you only see tracks shared by yourself and the users you follow.
- **User Profiles & Social Graph:** Built in following system allowing users to follow others and discover new music through their network.
- **Secure Authentication:** User login and session management powered by Supabase.

## Tech Stack

- **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (React)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **Database & Authentication:** [Supabase](https://supabase.com/)
- **API Requests:** Axios (Integrating with the Spotify API)
